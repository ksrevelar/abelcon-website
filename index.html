Our Services

Comprehensive construction solutions tailored to the Philippine landscape.

General Building

As a PCAB licensed General Building contractor (Category D), we handle vertical construction projects from foundation to finishing. Our expertise covers:

  • Residential Subdivisions
  • Mid-rise Commercial Buildings
  • Industrial Warehouses
  • Structural Retrofitting

Why Choose Us?

We strictly adhere to the National Building Code of the Philippines (PD 1096) and use only ASTM-standard materials.

Material Technology

We utilize elastomeric and cementitious waterproofing systems best suited for tropical climates.

  • Roof Deck Membrane Application
  • Toilet & Bath Waterproofing

  • Swimming Pool Sealing
